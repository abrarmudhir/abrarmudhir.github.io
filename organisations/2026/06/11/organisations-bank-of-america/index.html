About

Bank of America is a major financial services institution with a broad presence across consumer banking, wealth management, corporate banking, investment banking, markets, sales and trading, and risk management. The group operates at global scale and is widely associated with large, complex technology estates that support trading, analytics, client services, operations, and enterprise controls.

Within capital markets and investment banking, Bank of America has significant exposure to front-office and risk platforms spanning pricing, analytics, market data, and trade lifecycle infrastructure. Its technology environment is commonly associated with large-scale engineering across server-side services, risk systems, and cross-functional delivery involving business stakeholders, quantitative teams, and platform engineering.

For skills reference, Bank of America is often relevant where experience overlaps with Python-based backend engineering, financial markets platforms, derivatives pricing and risk concepts, and asset-class knowledge including equity derivatives such as futures, options, and convertibles. Publicly referenced domain associations also include pre-trade, origination, and post-trade workflows, as well as internal platforms such as Quartz within parts of the bank’s technology stack.
