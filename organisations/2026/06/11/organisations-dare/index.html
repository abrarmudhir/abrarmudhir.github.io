Organisations Dare

11 June 2026

Overview

An energy technology and trading company generating liquidity and value across global commodities markets through trading expertise, proprietary technology, and data science.

About

Dare describes itself as an energy tech company generating liquidity and value across global commodities markets. Its official site says the business combines deep trading expertise with proprietary technology and the power of data science, with an emphasis on understanding volatile, data-intensive markets and counterparty requirements.

As of June 11, 2026, Dare’s public site states that it is extending its trading expertise into new geographies and connected commodities markets. The company positions itself around a one-team culture, flat communication hierarchy, and an idea-meritocratic environment designed to challenge reasoning, reward excellence, and move quickly in fast-changing markets.

Dare’s current careers page highlights collaborative trading, technology, and business-function teams, and frames its technologists as building solutions intended to revolutionise global energy markets. The site lists its operating office at One America Square, Crosswall, London EC3N 2LS, while Companies House records Dare International Ltd as an active private limited company incorporated on April 22, 2016, with company number 10142406 and registered office at 93 Gloucester Place, London W1U 6JQ.
