      ProblemShared describes itself as an online mind health platform and a practitioner-led, CQC-regulated community of experts. Its public homepage says it works with individuals and organisations including NHS trusts and integrated care boards, universities, insurers, and private medical healthcare providers to deliver mental healthcare services and neurodevelopmental assessments for autism, ADHD, and specific learning difficulties, alongside post-diagnostic support.<br /><br />

      As of June 24, 2026, ProblemShared's public careers site says the company was founded in 2020, has completed more than 100,000 sessions, and has 350+ co-workers. The same careers content describes the organisation as a fully digital, remote-first platform connecting people across the UK with timely mental health and neurodevelopmental services, while the About page says the business has supported thousands of clients and combines people, data, and technology to broaden access to safe and effective care.<br /><br />

      ProblemShared's About and Careers pages state that founder and CEO Nick Nabarro started the company after seeing the impact of limited mental health resources and long waitlists during his work as an A&E doctor. Public site content emphasises evidence-based, person-centred, and neuroaffirming care, with service lines spanning ADHD and autism assessments, talk therapy, education navigation, psychoeducation, and ADHD medication prescribing where appropriate.<br /><br />
