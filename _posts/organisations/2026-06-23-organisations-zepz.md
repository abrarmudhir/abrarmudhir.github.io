      Zepz positions itself as a global payments group focused on fair, fast, and flexible cross-border money movement.       As of June 23, 2026, Zepz's public homepage says the group processed USD 10 billion of customer transfers in 2020, handled 50 million customer transactions in 2020, and supports more than 5,000 money transfer corridors. The same page says Zepz has over 800 colleagues, offices worldwide, and teams drawn from many nationalities, while its brand pages describe WorldRemit as serving transfers to more than 130 countries and Sendwave as a remittance product focused on Africa and Asia.<br /><br />

      Zepz's public careers and news pages describe the business as operating a unified global platform across its remittance brands. A March 9, 2026 leadership article on the company's site states that Barrie Morris stepped into the role of Interim Chief Executive Officer, having previously served as CFO since 2023 and been with Zepz since 2021. The same article says Zepz had reached cash-flow profitability, strengthened financial discipline, and expected send volumes and revenues to grow by more than 20 percent in 2026 as it expanded its product offering and footprint.<br /><br />
